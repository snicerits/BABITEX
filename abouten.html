About BABITEX

BABITEX is a tourism company based in Babite. BABITEX helps tourists to find and view the best viewing points in Babite by using a guide.

BABITEX offers to view several viewing sites based in Babite. The viewing objects are specifically selected so that customers can view Babite from all of the angles. Objects are arranged in a special order in the site's object section, from the most popular to the less popular.

BABITEX also offers its guide services. You can choose between 3 different price plans that you can view on the priceing page. People who don't want a guide have the option to buy the BASIC plan and use a virtual guide.
