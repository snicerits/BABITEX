Welcome to BABITEX

What is BABITEX?

BABITEX is a tourism company based in Babite. BABITEX helps tourists to find and view the best viewing points in Babite by using a guide.

Top Objects

Via Jurmala Outlet Village

Outlet village with different types and brand stores, at good prices. The only shop of this kind in Latvia. It is very popular and visited mostly during the summer period.

Saint Nicolai Lutheran Church

Architectural monument of national importance, which has played an important role in the history of Latvia.

Babīte Secondary School

One of the best schools in Latvia, with a modern interior.

The Babite Cultural Education Centre

The Babite Cultural Education Centre is a unique place in Babite, which includes Babite Music School and Babite Library.

Piņķi water reservoir, or "Gurķīts"

"Gurķīts" is the most visited place in Babite during the warm months of the year, as it enables relaxation and training in water skiing.

INBOX hockey arena

The INBOX hockey arena is a popular place, for the athletic Babite residents who regularly take the opportunity to skate in the evenings of Saturdays.

BABITEX cooperation with Babite municipality

Babitex is actively involved in the life of the municipality, supports and helps to organise the following events:

  • Festive events
  • Sporting events
  • Schools and Schools Self-Government events
  • and others

BABITEX believes that many people of Latvia do not know how beautiful and full of surprises Babite is, so we actively support Babite and promote its name in Latvia and the world. We're categorically against joining Marupe.
