Saint Nicolai Lutheran Church

Architectural monument of national importance, which has played an important role in the history of Latvia. Saint Nicolai Lutheran Church, known as the Church of Piņķi, is a relatively popular object, among Babīte residents. The object is temporarily closed to visitors during the pandemic, but its external viewing is not prohibited. The church has very beautiful architecture, in our tours, we analyze the architecture and talk about its history.

Via Jurmala Outlet Village

Via Jurmala Outlet Village is a store complex and a relatively new facility in Babīte . This facility is very popular in summer. The Via Jurmala Outlet Village is partially closed during the pandemic, yet some stores continue to operate. During our tour customers are given some free time to buy something.

The Babite Cultural Education Centre

The Babite Cultural Education Centre is a unique place in Babite, which includes Babite Music School and Babite County Library. The Babite Cultural Education Centre is a very popular place, especially among young people. The tour looks at the music school and the library. The music school is a very modern building. On the tour special attention is paid to the hall where concerts are held because it is modern and unique.

Babīte Secondary school

One of the best schools in Latvia, with modern decoration. Babīte Secondary School has a large number of cool and exiting sites, which includes, the school's modern concert room and its swimming pool.
