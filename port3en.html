“LEAX” plant

The Leax plant is one of the many plants in Babite and is one of the most advanced, equipped with a lot of different modern robots that perform human work. The tour looks at the production site and Leax representatives respond to customer questions.

Babite Secondary Schools Stadium and skatepark

Babite's Secondary School stadium, perhaps not as modern as the big stadium, but has its advantages, has a skatepark and unique treadmills. In the tour, we talk about details of the stadium.

Boiler house

The boiler house may not sound interesting initially, yet there is a unique opportunity to look at the new and old boiler house to see how quickly technology develops. The tour looks at the old and new boiler house and compares them.
